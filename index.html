About

Welcome to OBJECT DETECTOR STOLEN OR NOT. Our website is at your service if you want to buy a used item, whether it's a smartphone, PC, tablet, bicycle, TV, or anything else. Our site detects whether the item you're interested in has already been stolen or not and whether the seller or point of sale is reliable or not. You can also report a theft to raise awareness and discourage people from buying the item, encouraging them to return it to its rightful owner.
